It's going to be an "Elderberry Wine" kind of summer. A summer on the fields of the Duke Campus Farm, scratching sets in the WXDU studio, or figuring out the Ross Rack, eating strawberries off the vine, wringing the sweat off nitrile gloves. The sweet, sweet heat of summer. My electric guitar, on a porch, fed into my blues pedal. Listening to Wednesday and Waxahatchee and making music. Making friends, going to shows, catching bugs. Mothing, kayaking, bouldering. Writing vignettes, painting worlds, sketching leaves. I can't wait.

It strikes me that this must all be enabled by owning a car. Curse this flat country.
